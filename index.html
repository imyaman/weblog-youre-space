MY CONSOLE

Hello Google Cloud Platform Customer,

As you have already been notified earlier this year, your Google Cloud Platform (GCP) billing account(s) will be migrated to Google Cloud Korea LLC and KRW payments as the billing in your local currency, South Korean won (KRW) is now available. All customers with a South Korea address are required to be billed under Google Cloud Korea LLC.

We noticed that your account has not been migrated to your local currency, KRW. It’s important that you migrate your accounts to Google Cloud Korea LLC and KRW currency as soon as possible, since non-migrated accounts will be terminated by March 21, 2021.

Please note that your GCP resources or your pricing will not be affected by this migration, and will continue to run as usual.

What do I need to do?

Take a few minutes to migrate your GCP accounts, including any free tier or free trial accounts, to local currency payments. All customers with a South Korea address must do so to avoid future account termination.

To migrate your accounts, click on your GCP billing account(s) listed below and follow the instructions in your GCP console (you can find detailed instructions in the GCP Korea Migration FAQ listed below). If you are a registered business, you will need to have your business registration number and related tax information ready for tax-related purposes:

  • Billing Account: 01CDB7-731D1D-74DC29

If your unmigrated GCP billing account is terminated, your data on GCP will be deleted as set out in the Data Processing and Security Terms (Section 6.2). You are responsible for exporting your data on GCP that you wish to retain before expiration or termination.

Google enables you, in a manner consistent with the functionality of GCP services, to export your data. Data exporting depends on how you have chosen to store your data.

  • For exporting your data on Compute Engine, you can export/import an entire VM image in the form of a .tar archive. Find more information on images and storage options on our Compute Engine Documentation page.
  • For exporting your data on Big Query service, please see ‘Exporting table data’.
  • To review other services you are using, we recommend you to check the Google Cloud Documentation or you can contact Google Cloud Support for your questions about the functionality of GCP services.
What do I need to know?

When you migrate your GCP billing accounts:

  • The contracting party of your GCP, Google Maps Platform services, if applicable, will change to Google Cloud Korea LLC as a reseller (with offices located at Gangnam Finance Center 20fl., 152 Teheran-ro, Gangnam-gu, Seoul).
  • The currency of your account will change to KRW. Your pricing will not change.
  • Google Asia Pacific Pte Ltd will assign and transfer your Google Cloud Platform License Agreement and your Google Maps Platform License Agreement, if applicable, to Google Cloud Korea LLC, as Google Asia Pacific’s reseller. Your account information, including your email address and other contact information associated with it, will be transferred to Google Cloud Korea LLC.
  • Google Cloud Korea LLC will issue your invoices.
  • In accordance with local Korea laws, these invoices will be subject to Value Added Tax (VAT).
Need more information or help?
  • Learn more about local currencies and exchange rates.
  • For more information, please read the GCP Korea Migration FAQ.
  • If you have any questions or require assistance, please do not hesitate to contact Google Cloud Billing Support.

Thank you for being a valued Google Cloud Platform customer.
CONTACT GOOGLE CLOUD BILLING SUPPORT
Sincerely,
The Google Cloud Platform Team
