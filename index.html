Hello Google Cloud Platform Customer,

We’re sending this message to let you know about further updates that have been made to our listing of Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Subprocessors. We are adding:

  • Fujitsu Communication Services Limited (relisting)
  • PVP Inc

These subprocessors are third-party companies to Google that are authorized to provide services which include, but are not limited to, phone and email support, response, diagnosis and resolution services. We verify that our service providers have the required technical expertise, and that they maintain our security and privacy standards. We work only with trusted support service providers.

No action is required on your part.

Thank you for being a valued GCP customer.
