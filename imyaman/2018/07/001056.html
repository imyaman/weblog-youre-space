New Third-Party Subprocessor for Firebase
MY CONSOLE

Hello Firebase Customer,

We are sending this message to update you on the status of third-party subprocessors who provide customer and technical support for Firebase. No action is required.

To support you when you need help, we work only with trusted support service providers. We will soon add the following entity to our list of third-party subprocessors:

  • EPAM Systems, Inc

This subprocessor will provide services which include, but are not limited to, phone and email support; response, diagnosis and resolution services; incident tracking; labeling and transcription; and responding to customer queries. We verify that our service providers have the required technical expertise, and that they maintain our security and privacy standards.

You can view the current list of Firebase Third Party Subprocessors at any time.
