Welcome to Actions on Google, Sangyong!
You're now part of a community of Google Assistant developers. Start developing actions to engage users on devices like Google Home, Pixel, and Android phones. Questions? Contact our Support team anytime, and don't hesitate to ask questions in our developer communities.
We're excited to see what you build!
The Actions on Google Team
How to get started:
1
Learn how to design simple, engaging conversations
Designing conversational actions that are simple and engaging is tough! Learn from our conversational design experts about best practices for designing actions.
2
Develop
Visit the console and check out the different ways you can build actions. Pick a tool to get started on developing, and don't forget to use the documentation to learn more about Actions on Google.
3
Join the developer community
Join your fellow Actions on Google developers in Google+ and StackOverflow to ask questions and get tips on development.
4
Test and publish!
Make sure to read through the launch checklist and test your Assistant app. Once you're confident with your Assistant app, publish it to users!
5
Track analytics
Once you've published your Assistant app, you can track analytics for it in the Actions on Google console.
