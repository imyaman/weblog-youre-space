Hi Firebase Customer,

We are writing to let you know about a new data privacy setting for your Firebase project(s).

  • On January 28, 2021, Firebase added a new data privacy setting that allows you to control whether your Firebase Service Data is used to provide you with more in depth analysis, insights, and recommendations about non-Firebase Google services and to improve non-Firebase Google services.
  • You can learn more about this new setting and Firebase Service Data in our Privacy and Security documentation and adjust this setting from your Firebase project’s data privacy settings page (linked below).

Below is a list of your projects that are affected by this change, in case you want to review the details:

  • pwa001 (pwa001-4c3dc)
  • myhealthdata-teasing (myhealthdata-teasing)
  • pacificmedia (pacificmedia-38170)
  • blog (blog-c227a)
  • devopsdays (devopsdays-3b475)
  • weblog (weblog-683a9)

If you have any questions about the new setting, check our FAQ page here.

Sincerely,
Swetha on behalf of the Firebase team
