Dear Actions on Google Developer,
 

We're making some important changes to our Actions for Families (A﻿F﻿F) Program. As part of our continued effort to offer a family-friendly experience, starting Febr﻿ua﻿ry 1﻿1, 2﻿02﻿1 the program will be limited to developers with demonstrated experience in creating family-friendly content, by having a Teacher Approved app on Google Play.

 

If you do not have an Action in the Actions for Families program, you do not need to take any action.

 

If you currently have an Action in the Actions for Families program, you must submit a new version in the Actions on Google Console after filling out information about your Teacher Approved app in the Brand Verification section of the AoG Console:
1.  Add your domain url
2.  Add the Google Play webpage for your Teacher Approved app
3.  Submit your action for review
 

In the event you cannot provide the above by Feb﻿rua﻿ry 1﻿1, 202﻿1, you will be required to remove any child-directed content and opt-out of the Actions for Families program by visiting the Actions on Google Console and turning off the For Families toggle under Deploy > Directory Information > Additional information. Then, resubmit your modified Action for review. If your Action has not been resubmitted with the above steps by Febr﻿uary 1﻿1, 202﻿1, we will have to take your Action down.

 

We are immensely grateful for all of the hard work you have put into Actions on Google to date. Please reach out to the Support team if you have any questions.

 

Sincerely,
