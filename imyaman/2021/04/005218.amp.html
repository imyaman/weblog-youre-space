Your Google Cloud Platform account has been terminated as of March 21, 2021. Please sign up with Google Cloud Korea.
MY CONSOLE

Hello Google Cloud Platform Customer,

We made several attempts to reach out to you requesting to migrate your account to Google Cloud Korea LLC. All customers with a South Korea address are required to be under Google Cloud Korea LLC. As a result, your billing account has been terminated.

You have an opportunity to continue to use Google Cloud Platform by signing up with your local Google Cloud Provider -Google Cloud Korea, LLC.

Once your unmigrated GCP billing is terminated, your data on GCP will be deleted as set out in the Data Processing and Security Terms (Section 6.2). You are responsible for exporting your data on GCP that you wish to retain before expiration or termination.

Google enables you, in a manner consistent with the functionality of GCP services, to export your data. However, data exporting depends on how you have chosen to store your data.

  • For exporting your data on Compute Engine, you can export/import an entire VM image in the form of a .tar archive. Find more information on images and storage options on our Compute Engine Documentation page.
  • For exporting your data on Big Query service, please see ‘Exporting table data’.
  • For other services you are using, you may find information you need at https://cloud.google.com/docs or contact Google Cloud Support for your questions about the functionality of GCP services.
Need more information or help?

For more information, please read the GCP Korea Migration FAQ. If you have any questions or require assistance, please do not hesitate to contact Google Cloud Billing Support.

Thank you for being a valued Google Cloud Platform customer.
