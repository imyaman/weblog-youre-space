Hello Google Cloud Platform Customer,

We’re sending this message to let you know about further updates that have been made to our listing of Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Subprocessors.

With the objective of providing further transparency of our Subprocessors, we are now incorporating company registration numbers, registration addresses and parent companies for both our Third-Party Subprocessors and Google Group Subprocessors.

We are also adding:

  • an additional Telus affiliate, Transactel El Salvador, S.A. de C.V.
  • an additional Sellbytel affiliate, SELLBYTEL GROUP S.L.

No action is required on your part.

Thank you for being a valued GCP customer.
