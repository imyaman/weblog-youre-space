Updates to the Google Cloud Platform Third-Party Subprocessor list.
MY CONSOLE

Hello Google Cloud Platform Customer,

We’re sending this message to let you know about some changes to our listing of Third-Party Subprocessors who provide customer and technical support for Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

Our listing now includes additional information about our subprocessors, including details of the affiliates that they use, the products that they support, and the scope of their activities. We are also:

  • updating the listing of Intelenet Global Services to Teleperformance Global Services Private Limited, due to a corporate change to that company;
  • updating the listing of Cognizant Technology Solutions U.S. Corporation to Cognizant Worldwide Limited due to a corporate change to that company; and
  • updating the listing of Sigma Technologies, S.L. to Sigma Technologies, SLU due to a corporate change to that company.

We verify that our service providers have the required technical expertise, and that they maintain our security and privacy standards. We work only with trusted support service providers.

No action is required.

Thank you for being a valued GCP customer.
