Exciting upgrades to Action voices
 ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌  ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ ‌ 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
Dear Actions on Google Developer,
Starting March 4th, we'll begin upgrading certain Actions on Google voices to Wavenet technology. If your Action's voice is being upgraded, you can expect an improvement in its quality.
Will this change affect me?
If your Action's voice and locale combination is in the list below, the voice of your Action will be affected. If your Action's voice and locale combination isn't present, there will be no change to the voice of your Action.
4 new voices: Male (1 and 2), Female (1 and 2):
• English (en-US, en-GB, en-AU)
• French (fr-FR, fr-CA)
• German (de-DE)
• Russian (ru-RU)
• Korean (ko-KR)
• Polish (pl-PL)
1 new voice (Female 1):
• Japanese (ja-JP)
• Italian (it-IT)
• Portuguese (Brazil) (pt-BR)
• Dutch (nl-NL)
• Swedish (sv-SE)
You can listen to the new voices here.
What do I need to do?
You do not need to take any action. The voice upgrade will be performed from March 4 to March 11 and does not require any effort on your part. Just sit back and enjoy an improved voice for your Action if it is being upgraded.
Need more information or help?
If you have any questions or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact Actions on Google Support following this link.
Sincerely,
The Actions on Google Team
