To migrate your billing account, you can follow the steps below:

1. Click on the link to be redirected to the Migration page [1].
2. Click ‘Migrate’ option beside your billing account ID.
3. On the next page, you can update your payments profile and Tax ID (if needed).
4. Save.

For more information about this change, you can read the GCP Korea Account Migration FAQ through the link below [2].
